Creative Testing Framework

A hypothesis-driven approach to ad creative for MentorVerse AI. This framework was designed to systematically discover the most resonant messaging for our target audience of NEET PG aspirants.

Test 01: Online Coaching Ad Group

Central Question: What drives the initial click? A logical appeal to structure and technology, or an emotional appeal to overcoming procrastination?

Variation A: The Logical Appeal

Ad • www.mentorverse.ai/coaching

AI-Powered NEET PG Coaching

Get a structured, day-by-day study plan. Our AI tracks your progress & adapts to you. Human mentors for tricky concepts. Start your free trial.

Variation B: The Emotional Hook

Ad • www.mentorverse.ai/coaching

The Fun Way to Ace NEET PG

Stop procrastinating. Turn your prep into a game. Earn points, badges & rewards for completing goals. Stay motivated. Start your free trial now!

Test 02: Mock Tests Ad Group

Central Question: Do users respond better to a list of features (what it is) or the ultimate benefit (what it does for them)?

Variation A: The Feature Focus

Ad • www.mentorverse.ai/mock-tests

Realistic NEET PG Mock Tests

Experience the real exam pressure with our full-length mock tests. Huge question bank included. Get detailed performance analysis to improve.

Variation B: The Benefit Focus

Ad • www.mentorverse.ai/mock-tests

Ace Your Exam With Confidence

Don't just practice, simulate the real exam. Our in-depth analysis helps you pinpoint weaknesses and boost your score. Feel prepared on exam day.

Test 03: Study Material Ad Group

Central Question: Is the primary value proposition the quality of the content or the efficiency it provides?

Variation A: The Quality Angle

Ad • www.mentorverse.ai/materials

All-in-One NEET PG Study Material

Get comprehensive notes, summaries, and question banks covering every subject for the NEET PG exam. Meticulously curated by top experts.

Variation B: The Efficiency Angle

Ad • www.mentorverse.ai/materials

Stop Wasting Time Searching

Get all the high-yield notes and Q-banks you need in one place. Your complete NEET PG library. Stop feeling overwhelmed and start studying smarter.

Test Hypotheses Summary

Online Coaching

The 'Emotional Hook' ad will achieve a higher CTR and lower CPA by directly addressing the student's internal struggle with procrastination.

Mock Tests

The 'Benefit Focus' ad will outperform by connecting the feature of mock tests to the ultimate student goal: confidence and exam readiness.

Study Material

The 'Efficiency Angle' will generate more conversions by solving a more immediate pain point—the overwhelming and time-consuming search for good materials.

Primary Success Metric: For all tests, the primary KPI is Conversion Rate (Trial Sign-ups), with CTR as a key leading indicator of message resonance.
